# Training Material Outline: Modernization Enablement Program

## Audience Tracks
- CXO and business sponsors
- Enterprise architects and engineering leaders
- Delivery teams (developers, testers, SRE/operations)

## Module 1: Executive Summary and Business Case
- Why modernization now
- Risk, cost, and value framing
- KPI model and expected outcomes

## Module 2: Technical Architecture Fundamentals
- Domain-driven architecture for legacy transformation
- Service boundaries and ownership model
- API gateway, event bus, and coexistence adapters

## Module 3: Migration Plan and Delivery Governance
- Four-phase roadmap (Preparation, Coexistence, Transformation, Cutover)
- Entry/exit criteria and rollback patterns
- Governance cadence and decision rights

## Module 4: Engineering Practices
- Contract testing and integration quality gates
- Observability (logs, metrics, traces, SLOs)
- Security engineering in hybrid legacy-modern stacks

## Module 5: Data and Cutover Readiness
- Data migration waves and reconciliation
- Operational rehearsals and failback drills
- Runbook authoring and incident playbooks

## Module 6: Change Management and Capability Building
- Role transition plans
- Skill uplift roadmap (COBOL-to-modern stack pathways)
- Communication model for business and IT stakeholders

## Assessments and Certification
- Architecture review case study
- Migration risk simulation
- Production readiness checklist walkthrough

## Deliverables
- Slide deck per module
- Instructor notes and workshop scripts
- Hands-on lab guide for coexistence and cutover scenarios
